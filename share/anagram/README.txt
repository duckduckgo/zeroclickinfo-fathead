Anagram plugin for Duck Duck Go

Dependencies:
python - http://www.python.org/getit/

Examples:
# finds all the anagrams of airplane with a length of 5 (default)
`python parse.py anagram airplane`
# finds all the anagrams of "duck duck go" with a length of 6
`python parse.py anagram "duck duck go" 6`
# finds all the anagrams of airplane with a length between 4 and 10
`python parse.py anagram airplane 4 10`
